Hi Andrey,

You can do whatever you like with avr-usb, as long as you publish  
derived work under the GPL. Bundling it with a prototyping board is  
OK, even if you sell it. A version with Russian documentation is also  
OK, as long as you publish it for free.

If you make a Russian version available on your web site, please mail  
us an URL so that we can link it.
